Pixel Monsters Adventures

Pixel Monsters Adventures is a Pokemon Style RPG Game where you (The Player) start your journey with your very own personal Pixel Monster. You can choose to Collect, Battle or Trade these Monsters with other Players and Characters around the vast world of Pixel Monsters. As the player, you can choose how to modify your home, character and even your Pixel Monsters with In-Game Cosmetics.
(No Micro-Transactions)
